Tata Online Retail - Power BI Project – Business Scenario Analysis

**Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing an online retail dataset to derive actionable insights for senior leadership. The objective is to support strategic decision-making by identifying key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and expansion opportunities.

**The analysis is designed from two perspectives:**
•	CEO (Operations & Growth)
•	CMO (Marketing & Customer Insights)

Business Objective
•	Evaluate current business performance
•	Identify major contributors to revenue
•	Analyze customer and regional trends
•	Provide data-driven recommendations for expansion strategy

Key Business Questions
CEO Perspective (Operations & Strategy)
1.	What are the monthly revenue trends for 2011, and are there any seasonal patterns?
2.	Which regions/countries show the highest demand, excluding the United Kingdom?
3.	What factors are contributing to revenue growth or decline over time?
4.	Which markets present the best opportunities for business expansion?

CMO Perspective (Marketing & Customers)
1.	Which are the top 10 revenue-generating countries (excluding the UK), and how does quantity sold compare?
2.	Who are the top 10 customers by revenue, and how is revenue distributed among them?
3.	What customer segments contribute the most to overall sales?
4.	How can marketing efforts be optimized to retain high-value customers?

Data Cleaning
Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned using the following rules:
•	Removed records where Quantity < 1
•	Removed records where Unit Price < 0
•	Ensured data consistency for accurate insights

Dashboard & Visualizations
🔹 Q1: Monthly Revenue Trend (2011)
•	Visual: Line Chart
•	Insight: Identifies seasonality and revenue fluctuations over time
🔹 Q2: Top 10 Countries by Revenue (Excl. UK)
•	Visual: Bar Chart
•	Insight: Compares revenue and quantity sold across key markets
🔹 Q3: Top 10 Customers by Revenue
•	Visual: Sorted Bar Chart (Descending)
•	Insight: Highlights high-value customers for targeted retention
🔹 Q4: Global Demand Analysis (Excl. UK)
•	Visual: Filled Map / Heat Map
•	Insight: Identifies high-demand regions for expansion

Tools & Technologies
•	Power BI
•	Excel / CSV Dataset

Key Insights
•	Revenue shows clear seasonal trends, useful for forecasting
•	A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue
•	Certain countries demonstrate high demand and growth potential
•	Data-driven targeting can improve customer retention and marketing ROI

How to Use
1.	Download the .pbix file from this repository
2.	Open using Power BI Desktop
3.	Explore each dashboard tab corresponding to business questions

Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data can be transformed into strategic insights by aligning analysis with business leadership perspectives. It highlights the importance of combining operational and marketing views to drive growth and expansion.
